Meatballs Recipe

Meatballs are the sweetest and nice combi of food.It is a very easy and fast recipe one could like to cook anytime they want to.

Ingridients

  • Minced meat
  • Onions
  • Cooking Oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Corriander
  • Salt

    • Steps

    1. Put the minced meat on the cooking pan and leave it to cook for some time.
    2. Put some chopped onions.
    3. Put little cooking oil for theonions to cook until brown.
    4. Put a spoonfull of tomato paste.
    5. Put some chooped tomatoes
    6. Add corriander
    7. Add some salt to bring it to taste. leave it to simmer for sometime.
    8. Serve it while hot
