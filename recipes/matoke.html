Matoke

It is a traditional banana based dish in most parts of africa.It is a regular meal bearing different names in each region in the world.There is no doubt that once you finish your plate of matoke,youll find yourself yearning for more.There are diffent ways of preparing matoke .

Ingridients

  1. Matoke
  2. Onions
  3. Cooking Oil
  4. Tomatoes
  5. plain yorghut
  6. Corriander
  7. Salt

Steps

  1. Chop some matoke that you want to cook
  2. Put some choped onions
  3. Put some cooiking oil and leave it to cook until brown
  4. Put 2 chooped tomatoes
  5. Put some salt
  6. Put two spoons of plain yorghut and let it simmer
  7. Put your matoke and leave it to cook until its ready
  8. Put chopped corriander and leave to cook for some time until its ready to serve
