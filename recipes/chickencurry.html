Ingridients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Plain yorghut
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Corriander

    • Steps

    1. Put the chicken things on a frying pan
    2. Put enough oil
    3. Put your onions and let it fry until golden brown
    4. Simmer your tomatoes and let them cookfor a while
    5. Put your tomato paste
    6. Add three spoons of plain yorghut and leave it for sometime to boil
    7. Add some little water
    8. Add some salt
    9. Put your corriander and leave it to simmer antuil its ready to serve
